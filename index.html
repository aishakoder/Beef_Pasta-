Beef Pasta

Ingredients

  • Meat loaf 500g
  • 1 Onion in cubes
  • 2 tbsp GG Paste
  • 1 tsp Cummin
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Chilli powder
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli flake
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 500 g Pasta

Directions

  1. Cut the onions in cubes, and fry them golden brown
  2. After the onions are ready add in GG paste and give it a good mix 2-3 min
  3. Add in the meat loaf and cook it until it turns brown
  4. After the meat loaf is cooked add all the spices
  5. Add in the pasta sauce in and give it a good mix
  6. Now that pasta sauce is ready let it simmer on lowest heat
  7. Take a new pot fill it halfvay up with water and boil the pasta 10-15 min
  8. Drain the pasta and give it a good rinse.
  9. Add the pasta in the pasta sauce and give it a good mix.
  10. Serve the pasta with some grated cheese. Bon Appetit
